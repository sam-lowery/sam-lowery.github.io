The tutors you need, all in one place.

Falcon Tutor is a free web-based service that enhances the opportunity for academic success amongst cadets. The platform connects cadets across the cadet wing for tutoring services. Furthermore, it enables cadets to register as tutors across various academic departments and courses within those departments. The Air Force Academy has one of the most rigorous academic programs in the country, in part due to the exhaustive core curriculum. Consequently, cadets are constantly in search of help in many of their courses. Finding credible resources to aid in academic understanding and for completing assignments can be difficult. Falcon Tutor provides cadets with an avenue for finding credible help. Many squadrons already have unofficial lists of tutors within their squadron. Falcon Tutor serves as a more official platform for connecting cadets to tutors, not only within their own squadron, but across the entire wing. Cadets can access various features of the website through easy to use web forms. Cadets can individually create Falcon Tutor accounts where they can search for tutors. Specifically, Falcon Tutor,allows cadets to use filters when searching for tutors. These filters include departments, classed, and squadrons. Each cadet can also register as a tutor and add classes they are qualified to tutor for. Instructors may also register, allowing cadets to find other professors teaching their classes.
